Hi guys, my name is Jelena and I'm junior web developer and web designer. As Pele once said: "Success is no accident. It is hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice and most of all, love of what you are doing or learning to do."

Guided by those words and the love I have towards programming and design, every day I'm looking for ways to improve my knowledge and become excellent programmer and designer. My goal is to write a clean and sustainable code and to create simple and functional design in which everyone will enjoy.
